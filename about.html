About Ivana






Ivana Pazek is the author of the Beyond the Fairytale book series, as well as an agile coach, and the creator of Shanghai-based Quantum Career Coaching (QCC, or Shanghai Liangxi Human Resources Co., Ltd.). Ivana became interested in how we contribute to working in the best, or worst, conditions and how to improve, which is the company's objective. The importance of mindset agility and the healing of negative patterns as crucial for long-term beneficial change is emphasized in QCC programs.
