Meet Ivana






Ivana Pazek is a transformational leader, podcast host, and author known for the Two Corporate Personality Types theory. Starting her career in ICT, Ivana became deeply interested in how we work within organizations at various levels and in team dynamics.

This led to knowledge, research, and discovery into the role we each play in creating the best (or worst) working conditions, and how to (successfully) navigate and improve. In her books, Ivana emphasizes mindset agility and the 'inner' shift necessary for any sustainable 'outer' change.

In the summer of 2018, Ivana moved to China, Shanghai, to pursue her love for coaching and growth facilitation in multinational corporations.

Ivana is also a social media influencer, who moved from 500 to ~50000 followers in one year. Follow Ivana on personal and company Linkedin Page.
